## Fibonacci-specific coverage

The verified peer-reviewed recent core remains Brisaboa et al. (2021),
Pooksombat et al. (2022), and Shapira (2023). Unreviewed 2026
preprints found during the search are excluded from the manuscript
bibliography and positioning argument. The search did not identify a
peer-reviewed 2024--9 June 2026 paper that implements the same transformation:
Zeckendorf decomposition of an arbitrary byte-block integer, adjacent index
gaps, frequency-ordered gap alphabet, and complete raw fallback.

This search result is not used to claim priority. The paper states only that
the proposed representation differs operationally from the verified
Fibonacci universal-code and generalized-number-system literature.
