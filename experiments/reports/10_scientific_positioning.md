# Scientific Positioning

FISA 1.0 is positioned as:

> An exact analysis of a Zeckendorf-index gap transform for block integers,
> including a complete lossless format, side-information accounting,
> exhaustive small-integer characterization, and evidence identifying when
> representation-level gains disappear after serialization.

The contribution is an exact hybrid representation with a raw fallback and a
shared alphabet. Its scope is conditional block representation for sparse
Zeckendorf decompositions, supported by complete serialized-size accounting,
correctness proofs, and reproducible comparisons with integer codes and
general-purpose codecs.
